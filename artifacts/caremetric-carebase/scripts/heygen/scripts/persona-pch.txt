Running a Pennsylvania personal care home? Chapter 2600 expects twelve hours of training a year for every direct care worker, current RASP assessments and support plans, medication practicums, Act 34 clearances, and fire drill logs. Each one with its own documentation.

Here's the truth. Most homes don't get cited for skipping the work. They get cited because the sign-in sheet can't be found.

CareBase logs the hours as they happen, keeps the documentation attached, and has Chapter 2600 mapped for you. So you're ready long before the surveyor ever arrives.
